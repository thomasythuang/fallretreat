Frequently Asked Questions


Where is Fall Retreat?

Fall Retreat this year will be in beautiful Lake Geneva, Wisconsin. The camp we’ll be at has roughly a quadzillion things to do (rock climbing, paintball, sports, just to name a few), so leave your books and homework behind!

Wisconsin?! How do I get there from campus?

Don’t worry about that! We’ll arrange the transportation, just be ready to leave from campus Friday afternoon. If you have a class that goes late, just let us know! Return rides will also be coordinated to get you back to campus by Sunday afternoon.

What can I expect during Fall Retreat?

Tons of fun. Meaningful talks from a guest speaker. New friends. Old friends. New friends who are older. Spikeball. Time to get to know other people in your class. Sports. Hanging out late at night. Awesome worship. Discussion on how to grow in your relationship with Jesus. Food.

What do I bring?

A pillow, toiletries, a Bible, a pen, and whatever other clothes you need for running around or lounging around.
